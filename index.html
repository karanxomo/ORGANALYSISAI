Meet EcoBot Waste Manager

An AI-powered waste management system designed specifically for trains. EcoBot smartly detects and segregates waste beside train seats, automating the entire process to keep compartments clean, reduce manual effort, and promote eco-friendly travel.

🚨 PROBLEM

Keeping trains clean and managing waste manually is challenging, unhygienic, and inefficient due to mixed garbage and limited disposal options. Current systems require significant human intervention, creating health risks and reducing operational efficiency in busy rail networks.

🎯 MISSION

Automate waste segregation in trains to keep compartments clean, reduce manual effort, and promote eco-friendly travel. Our goal is to revolutionize onboard waste management through intelligent automation, making train journeys cleaner and more sustainable for everyone.
